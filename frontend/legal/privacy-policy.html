Privacy Policy

Brand Beat Inc. is sensitive to concerns that personal information collected from its customers and clients (“you” or “your”) be handled respectfully and appropriately.  

This privacy policy (the “Privacy Policy”) describes how your personal information is collected, used and shared when you visit, use or make a purchase from our website (the “Site”). Throughout this Privacy Policy, the terms “we”, “us”, and “our” refer to Brand Beat Inc.. (“Brand Beat”), and the terms “you” and “your” refer to the user of our Site.

‍

What Information Is Collected?

‍

When you visit the Site, we automatically collect certain information from your device, including without limitation information about your web browser and I.P. address. As you browse the Site, we may collect information about the individual web pages or products that you view, what websites or search terms referred you to the Site, and information about how you interact with the Site (such collected information is hereinafter referred to as the “Device Information”).

We may use “cookies” on our Site. A cookie is a piece of data stored on a Site visitor’s hard drive, which may be used by us to help us improve our Site and your access to it, and which identifies repeat visitors to our Site. Cookies also enable us to track and target the interests of our users to enhance their browsing experience. Our utilization of cookies is in no way linked to any personally identifiable information on our Site.

If you make a purchase or attempt to make a purchase through our Site, we may collect certain information from you, including without limitation your name, billing address, shipping address, payment information (including credit card information), email address, and phone number (such information is hereinafter referred to as “Order Information”).

In this Privacy Policy, “Personal Information” shall mean both Device Information and Order Information.

‍

How We Use Your Personal Information

We use the Order Information that we collect to, among other things, communicate with you and fulfill and process any orders placed through the Site (including without limitation processing your payment information and providing invoices and/or order confirmations). 

We use the Device Information that we collect to help us screen for potential risks and, more generally, to improve and optimize our Site (for example, by generating analytics about how our customers browse and interact with the Site).

Right to Access and Rectify Personal Information:

Under the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (Canada) and, where applicable, similar provincial privacy legislation, you are entitled to a copy of the personal information contained in your client file by means of a written request. You may verify this personal information and request that any inaccurate information be corrected by contacting us through one of the ways herein described.

‍

Your Access to and Control Over Information

You may opt out of any future contact or communication from us or request that we discontinue sending emails and other communications to you at any time by contacting the Privacy Officer in the manner set out within this Privacy Policy.

‍

Security

Brand Beat takes precautions to protect your information. When you submit sensitive information via the Site, we use reasonable commercial efforts to protect your information both online and offline.

Wherever we collect sensitive information, that information is encrypted and transmitted to us by secure servers. We may include common indications of such secured features when appropriate, such as a closed lock icon at the bottom of your web browser.

While Brand Beat uses encryption to protect sensitive information transmitted online, we also protect your information offline. Only employees who need the information to perform a specific service (for example, billing or customer service) are granted access to your personally identifiable information. The computers/servers in which we store personally identifiable information are kept in a secure environment.

‍

Third Parties

‍Please be aware that some of Brand Beat’s business partners may use cookies or other methods of tracking and targeting on their respective sites (advertisers, for example).  However, by using our Site you acknowledge that Brand Beat has no access to or control over such cookies and such sites and this Privacy Policy does not extend to the sites of such third parties.  Please be aware that we are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of such third parties. We encourage you to, when you leave our Site, read the privacy statements of any other site that may collect your personal information.

‍

Updates

Our Privacy Policy may change from time to time and all updates will be posted on this page. You may not be contacted directly upon a change to our Privacy Policy

‍

Privacy Officer

If have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you may contact our Privacy Officer via email at hello@brandbeat.ca
